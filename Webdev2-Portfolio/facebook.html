Facebook

Introduction

This app is mostly used for users to post to show others who they are friends with A user must add another to be able to see what they post.

To set up

The facebook appl is available on android and apple phones start by searching the app in each systems respective app store. Once open the application will beginYou will be asked for basic information such as your name, date of birth ,and gender. The end process is adding your mobile number or email adress , creating a password and finally agreeing to facebooks terms of service

How to Use the app

Facebook is used by people as a personal news site, where the things posted are about people they know and by themselves. The majority of people interact on a daily basis so when you log onto the app or website you will see status updates and photos from the people you are friends with on Facebook. To keep up to date with people you know and see what they are up to to you can send them a friend request, you do this by just typing in the person’s name in the search bar, and clicking the icon on their profile, saying “Add Friend”. The next option is join your companys workspace automatically using your work email address. The last option is find workspaces youre eligible to join by looking up your email address.

How can be helpful in learning

This app can be helpful with learning as students can communicate together to help each other. And also find news as in what business are hiring, if they become friends or like a page and the business post that they are hiring the student can see what requires are needed for the job by looking at the post

Cautions

Facebook is one of them apps where nothing you write is private. If you were to comment on a post made by someone, other people can see what you wrote and reply to that specific response. Second cautions is increase in cyberbullying online. This form of bullying is especially bad as people don’t feel they are in the wrong, or think about the persons feelings for the things they say or do as they are protected by a screen and it allows them to say things that they would never say in person. Fake accounts are also extremely dangerous. As there is no real verification method to go through when creating a Facebook account, there have been cases where people have made false accounts for malicious purposes. Such as a peadophile luring kids out in public or an account made to mock someone
