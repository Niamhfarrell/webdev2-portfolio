Instagram

Introduction

The main attraction around Instagram is users adding images about anything and write a comment about it. , the user needs to follow other users to be able to see their page and anything that the person they follow posts will come up on the main page. Instagram has recently added a new feature of being able to have a live video so their followers can join and see what the person is doing,

Set Up

The set up for the social media is simple, there is 4 steps, Firstly after downloading the app click the do not have an account and it will bring you to a page with information you will need to fill out such as name password and a username. After this it will bring you to your profile where you can start adding images.

How to Use the app

The app is simple to work, the user must follow people they can find them by looking them up in the search bar is know the followers name or suggested ones can come up on the explore page and can follow people off that. A user can like a person image but pressing the red heart and can comment. A can also add a story that will only last 24 hours a user can not comment or like a story. People can also tag a user in a picture.

how useful for leaning

This app is useful for learning as the lecturer could make a page that the students follow and could start a live video that students can view. Students are able to make pages for a certain course such as a photography page and can follow other well know photographers to get information and ask them questions.

Cautions

The first castion is when posting pictures,the user should keep in mind the location setting and the tags you use. These could easily show your location to people. Tags can also be used to see your interests and your location so use them with caution. The second is if you’re tagged in their posts or stories. You can un-tag your account from posts but you can’t from stories so you can ask the person to remove you from the story. Stories aren’t permanent so after 24 hours they get removed.
