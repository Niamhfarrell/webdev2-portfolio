Linkdin

Introduction

The purpose of this app to give people the opportunity to find a job and speak to an employer, and the employer to see the employees background and if they have the qualification for a job.

To set up

After downloading from the app store on a phone or laptop. When first time on the app the person must put in theor name and the college that they attended or currently studying in, the user must also add their email to be able to get notifications. Once logged in the set it up like many apps a person can make a profile, as it says on the app a person with a profile picture is 21 times greater of having a chance of getting employed than someone who dose not have a picture. Within the profile the person can add background information such as where from, what currently working as and what studying to allow the employer have the requirements they need for the job.

How to Use the app

The app also allows students to follow a specific job that they are interested in and any jobs that are available. It will appear on the main page once the user looks up the area that they are interested in. The employee will also get notifications when a job is available.Employees can also talk to employers

How useful for leaning

Linkedin is a useful app for learning as allows students have the opportunity to have the chance of finding a job or internship in the career that they are perhaps currently studying. It also allows them to communicate with the employer to ask questions and get an insite to see what working with a company will be like, it will give students confidence to talk to employers before start work.

Cautions

Like every app there is cautions, these include the following your name is available to many different companys and other people using the app, the users age and location is also available.
